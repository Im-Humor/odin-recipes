Cake

Description

Hello, and thank you for visiting my web page about [delicious food], I am glad that you have found this page and I hope that you will attempt to make [food].

Ingredients

  • Betty Crocker's Vanilla Cake
  • 2 cups of water
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to bowl, stir expeditiously for 5 minutes
  2. Pour batter into 10 inch cake round
  3. Microwave for 5 minutes
  4. Enjoy, human.
