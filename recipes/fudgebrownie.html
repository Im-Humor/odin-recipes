Fudge Brownies

Description

These gorgeous fudgie brownies were my great Aunt Jemimah's secret recipe. Don't mention this to anyone because otherwise she'll come back from the grave to haunt me.

Containing melty chocolate pieces, a gooey chocolate center, and just the right amount of crisp edges, I'd kill her again for these brownies. I said what I said.

Ingredients

  • 2 hershey bars, diced
  • 2 cups flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 13 tbsp salted butter - auntie always said the saltier the better!

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to pot
  2. Put pot over open flame, as if a witch brewing a potion to kill that good looking family down the street and turn them into your slaves
  3. Cook for 10 days
  4. Plate, and enjoy!
